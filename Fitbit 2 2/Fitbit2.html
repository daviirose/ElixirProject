What is fitbit?

Fitbit was founded in 2007 by James Park and Eric Friedman. Since the launch of the high-tech
fitness band in 2011, over 21 million devices have been sold. The band has many features including step
counts, calories burned and flights climbed. The band has an app that goes with it that can sync up to
a mobile device, which will record all the data while the band is being worn. As the popularity of the
active bands went up, the company started to release different models of the FitBit. Fitbit Flex and
FitBit Charge. Many people compare the FitBit to the Apple Watch. Although both fitness tools can
do essentially the same thing, studies show that many people prefer the Apple Watch over the FitBit.
There are several pro’s and con’s when thinking about buying a FitBit.

Fitbit only started to recieve positive recognition in 2009, people started to realize the pros of having a
fitbit and started paying more attention to it. A lot of people decided to try it out for themselves. The founder
of fitbit first raised $400,000 to start up the company from the ground up to realize it
wasnt enough. He then decided to attract investors to raise money for the company. People gave in
and started investing, before they knew it fitbit blew up. Nobody ever invented a product like this
one and that is the reason why it started getting so much recognition.

Finding Motivation!

By getting a FitBit you will have a chance to set up your health and fitness goals. Doing that will help you
be aware of how much cardio, sleep, calories you achieve. You can adjust your goals
anytime whether you want to do more or less. Fitbit will motivate you and push you to achieve
your goal every day. Knowing your goals will psychologically motivate you to get the
job done when you realize your goal is incomplete. Once you are done with your goal for the
day you will feel good about yourself, when you see progress you will strive for better
and before you know will reach your goal a lot quicker than expected.

Pros:

  • It is a great motivator, once you set a goal you are determined to achieve it until you get there. If you have not reached your goal by nighttime, you will have take the extra mile to reach your goal.
  • It has a silent alarm feature that allows you to wake up peacefully, without rushing to shut your alarm and without disturbing your partner, roommate or relatives.
  • It can track how many steps you take in a day, how many miles you walk or run, the food you eat and how many calories they have, and it also measures your blood pressure.

Cons:

  • Must connect with a smartphone or computer, to see your steps on a daily basis there are some cheap Fitbit apps you can check or you could sign in on your computer.
  • Not fully accurate when tracking the steps taken in a day.
  • Very over priced due the lack of feautures it comes with compared to the apple watch such as texting and talking on the phone.
